My most valuable asset and passion of mines for the past 3 years have been Intraday Trading. A love and hate relationship. Through years of practice, patience, and discipline, I have progressed to the point where I am now. In these markets, I am going head to head against billion dollar firms, Quants, and banks. Each day, I clock in at 5:30am, ready to make trade decisions with confidence in my edge in the market backed with historical data.

In this profession, deemed as the most mentally challenging job by many, I have not only grown skill-wise but also grown myself as person. What I have learned from this ruthless market is that it does not care about anyone or anything. Every day, the market has a goal: to reach a certain price, stopping anyone who decides to go against it. I have watched others online who fail to realize this key idea, get slaughtered in this market. I was one of those traders at one point...However, my ability to adapt, grow, and learn from these mistakes allowed me to reach the point where I have proven my consistency in taking valid and winning trades. I have faced plenty of days where I have taken losses back to back to back. Each time I enter a trade, it is my decision and my accountability. I have learned how to manage my risk, my emotions, my executions.

After 3 years, through the lows and the highs, I am glad to say I have finally gotten to the point where I am confident in the markets and myself. This same process that I have gone through is applicable to everything every person goes through in life. Whether it is games or Computer Science or a hobby. So many people get stuck in a phase where nothing is working or they are not good at it, they fail and give up. Through time and respecting the process, everyone can become great at it if enough effort and determination is shown.
